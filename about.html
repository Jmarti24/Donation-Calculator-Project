About Us

The story behind the Tax Calculator began as a walk around the Goodwill facility in San Bernardino. Interacting with a workforce that was predominantly dis-abled it occured to me that it became important to extend the notion of inclusivity to all platforms including technology. It came as no surprise that even the tech industry maintained outdated narratives and was in need of a new paradigm. So fueled only with inspiration and an attempt to implement change, came the idea that you see today.

Our Product

Tested on a focus groups through first and last ideation, as well as technical colleagues at every stage. When looking through similar donation sites, all that was found was confusing click down menus with tiny fonts and too much need for clicking. My focus group wanted easier usability, better accesibility, and shorter use time with less frustration to make their donations of cash and goods.
